About

Builder-architect across AI platforms and data engineering

I am a Senior Data & AI Engineer at PayPal in San Jose, focused on enterprise agentic systems (LangGraph, MCP), retrieval-augmented generation, anomaly detection, and LLM fine-tuning with strong governance and observability.

Before PayPal, I spent a decade in Bay Area data engineering — Spark and SQL pipelines at US Bank and Barclays via Kforce and Cognizant — with deep work in fraud analytics, feature engineering, and platform governance.

I bridge research and production through open source (CortexOps, TideVec) and publications on multi-agent coordination and energy-aware LLM optimization. Starting Fall 2026, I pursue a PhD in Computer Science (Cybersecurity & AI) at Dakota State University.
