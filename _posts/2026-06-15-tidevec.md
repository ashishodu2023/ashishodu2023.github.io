**TideVec** is a temporally-aware, causally-indexed vector database (C++20). Temporal decay and causal graphs keep RAG from ranking stale facts like fresh ones.

- Code: [github.com/ashishodu2023/TideVec](https://github.com/ashishodu2023/TideVec)
- Python SDK: [pypi.org/project/tidevec](https://pypi.org/project/tidevec/)
