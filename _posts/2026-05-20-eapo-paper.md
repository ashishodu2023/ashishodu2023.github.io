Published on TechRxiv: [Energy-Aware Prompt Optimization for Large Language Models](https://doi.org/10.36227/techrxiv.175615629.90190202/v1).

EAPO uses Bayesian optimization to design prompts that balance task quality with energy and token cost — up to large Tokens-per-Joule gains at iso-accuracy.
